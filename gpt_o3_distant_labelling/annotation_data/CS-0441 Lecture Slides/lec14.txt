Is strong induction somehow more powerful than
mathematical induction?
The ability to assume P(0) ‚àß P(1) ‚àß ‚Ä¶ ‚àß P(k) true before

Perhaps surprisingly, mathematical induction, strong
induction, and well ordering are all equivalent!

be written using the other two methods
